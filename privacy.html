Privacy Policy

At SAVEMATE, your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when using our financial management app.

1. Information We Collect

We collect both personal and non-personal information to provide you with the best possible service. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, student ID, payment information (if applicable).
  • Financial Data: Expenses, income, savings goals, and transaction history.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to provide core features and improve your experience. This includes:

  • Tracking your expenses and managing budgets.
  • Setting and monitoring savings goals.
  • Offering personalized financial advice and tips.
  • Providing customer support when needed.

3. Data Security

Protecting your data is a top priority. We use encryption and secure servers to ensure that your information is safe. Only authorized personnel have access to your data.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services to enhance the app's functionality, such as payment processing or analytics. Rest assured, these services follow strict confidentiality and security practices.

5. Your Rights

As a SAVEMATE user, you have the right to access, update, and delete your personal information. You can also opt-out of certain services, such as marketing communications.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in the law or our services. We encourage you to review this page regularly for updates.
